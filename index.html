Historic Road Preservation

Locust Avenue

To the Mayor, Township Committee & Traffic Safety Officials

We, the undersigned residents, property owners, and concerned citizens, respectfully petition for immediate action to preserve the safety, character, and historic integrity of Locust Avenue by implementing the following traffic safety measures:

  1. Designate Locust Avenue as a 4-ton weight limit road to reduce damage from heavy commercial vehicles and preserve the roadway's historic residential character.
  2. Install clearly marked Yield to Pedestrian crosswalks at the intersections of Linden Avenue and Osborne Avenue.
  3. Add appropriate pedestrian warning signage and traffic-calming measures to improve safety for residents, children, walkers, cyclists, and motorists.

Locust Avenue is an important historic and residential roadway and bridge that deserves protection from excessive truck traffic, speeding, and unsafe pedestrian crossings. Increased traffic volume and heavy vehicles have created concerns regarding roadway deterioration, pedestrian safety, noise, and the overall quality of life for residents.

We believe these proposed changes will:

  • Improve pedestrian safety
  • Reduce roadway wear and infrastructure damage
  • Preserve the historic and residential character of Locust Avenue
  • Encourage safer driving behavior
  • Protect families, children, and residents who regularly cross at Linden Avenue and Osborne Avenue

We respectfully request that the appropriate municipal authorities review this petition, conduct a traffic and safety study if necessary, and move forward with implementing these protections as soon as possible.
